Instructions for the Online Assessment

Key Guidelines

Number of Questions: The assessment includes 10 multiple-choice questions (MCQs).

Time Allocation: You have 30 minutes to complete the test. The timer will commence automatically when you start.

Navigation Instructions:

  • Save & Next: Saves your current answer and moves to the next question.
  • Submit: Finalizes your responses and submits the test. Post-submission, modifications will not be allowed.

Selecting Answers:

  • Each question has four options. Choose the correct answer by clicking the corresponding radio button.
  • You can update your selection at any time before submission.

Clearing Responses:

  • You may clear your selected answer by clicking the "Clear Response" button for any question, which will leave it unattempted.

Scoring:

  • Each correct answer earns 1 mark.
  • No marks are awarded for unattempted questions.
  • There is no negative marking for incorrect answers.

Final Score: Your score will be displayed upon completion and submission of the test.

Status Indicators:

Question visited but not yet saved.

Question saved successfully.

Question not yet visited.

Accepting Terms and Conditions:

  • You must accept the terms and conditions by checking the box before starting the exam.
  • The Start Exam button will remain inactive until the checkbox is marked.
  • Upon accepting the terms, the Start Exam button will become active, allowing you to proceed.

Beginning the Exam:

  • Clicking the Start Exam button will initiate the test and redirect you to the questions page.
  • Please ensure you are prepared, as the timer will start immediately.
